Human-Scene Reconstruction: A Review of Representations, Methods, and Embodied Applications

Anurag Gupta, Ziqi Mao, Samuel Gu

University of Glasgow

Report / BibTeX / GitHub

Overview

Human-scene reconstruction studies how to recover people and their surrounding environments in a shared 3D or 4D representation. This report reviews the core representations, reconstruction methods, datasets, and embodied applications behind that problem.

The survey is organized around the requirements of spatial consistency, temporal coherence, physical contact, and downstream usefulness for embodied agents. It connects human mesh recovery, scene reconstruction, dynamic reconstruction, and robotics-facing perception.
